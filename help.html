What the site does

This webpage explains how to use the website

Accessing the site

As you are reading this, you probably already have a browser and access to the internet, so typing http://3d-o-x.appspot.com into the url bar at the top of your browser or clicking will take you to the front page of my website. At this point, you will need a Google account to log in which can be obtained free from Google by clicking the sign up icon at the top right of the log in page.
Welcome Page

A modern browser is needed to make use of some of the features of the website. Examples of a modern browser include Google Chrome, Mozila Firefox and Internet Explorer version 9 or higher. In older browsers, you will not be able to see the cube in perspective.

Offline Viewing

Without internet access, you will not be able to use any part of this site except for downloading this help file for offline. To do this, download help.zip, save it in a new folder then extract the files.

Starting a game

If another player is online, you will see their name at the left of your screen when you are at your user homepage (the first page you see when you log in).
homepage
If you click on their name, you are taken to a page where you can select a timing for the game. Once you have chosen your desired time limit per move, click the "start game" button to tell your opponent theat you want to play a game against them. If they are not online, you will be redirected to your homepage in about 10 seconds, otherwise wait for them to respond.
making an offer
Someting similar to the notification at the bottom of the screen below will be shown to the opponent. If they click yes, you will both be redirected to a game page, if they click no, you will be redirected back to your user page.
